Extra Curricular Activities

In addition to the regular studies in the classes. Ganesh English Secondary school emphasizes on extracurricular activities for the all- round development of students. It includes quizzes, elocution contests, spelling contest, debates and poetry. Equal emphasis has been given to games and cultural activities. First hand practical knowledge regarding project writing for lower and secondary students are highly encouraged. The school is running day borders as well as borders with full-fledged residential facilities.

Excursion and picnics

During an academic year educational tour programm are organized from all Grades from Nursery to Grade 10. they are taken to various places (Historical, Geographical, Cultural and General spot) around Kathmandu valley.

Language

As the importance of the English language cannot be ignored in the context of globalization, the medium of teaching is English but the importance of Nepal aids the official language has equally been stressed Besides, Ganesh School aims to offers classes in other national language in future.

Freeships and Scholarships

To lighten the financial burden of the parents. Ganesh English Secondary school provides according to the government policy.
