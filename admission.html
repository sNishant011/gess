Admission Procedure

  • Admission is granted on the basis of the results of the entrance test and interview.
  • Admission forms will be distributed for classes Nursery to IX
  • A student can register his /her name after succeeding the entrance test.
  • Application form duly filled in should be returned to the school office accompanied by:
    • Birth certificate
    • Four Passport
    • Applicant's last school progress report and transfer certificate.
  • The written test will be taken in the following subjects:
    1. English
    2. Mathematics
    3. Science
    4. Nepali
    • Note: Question will be based on the standard of the class in which is the presently studying or has complete. We always welcome parents/guardians to visit Ganesh English Secondary School to discuss the academic performance of their children or any other point related to the children.
